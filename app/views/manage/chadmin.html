Chapter Administration

On the page, Chapter Managers can create reports for:

- Volunteers (This report will show how many hours have been reported and verified for each volunteer in your chapter)

- Volunteer comparison (This report will show how many volunteer hours your chapter has logged, and how many volunteers have logged hours compared to other chapters)

- Events (This report will show a complete view of all events put on by the chapter within a selected time frame)

Other Management functionality can be added based on organizational needs.

These reports should have a link to export to excel for more functionality.
